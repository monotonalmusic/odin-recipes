Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is sure to fill you and your family up. Rich and velvety, the classic italian dish is satisfying and easy to make with these simple steps.

  • ground beef
  • onions
  • garlic
  • italian seasioning
  • crushed tomato
  • tomato paste
  • lasagna noodles
  • mozzarella cheese
  • parmesan cheese
  • ricotta cheese
  1. Begin by making the red sauce. Cook down sausage, beef, onions and garlic, followed by your tomato ingredients. Add your seasoning now.
  2. Bring a large pot of water to boil. Follow the instructions on the pack, and cook your lasagna noodles accordingly.
  3. Pre-heat oven to 375F.
  4. Arrange meat sauce, then noodles, then ricotta cheese, then mozzarella cheese, repeat until you have all of the necessary layers.
  5. Bake in preheated oven with foil on top, approx 25 minutes.
  6. Take the foil off, and bake for 25 more minutes.
  7. Take the foil off, and bake for 25 more minutes.
  8. Done! Enjoy with your friends and/or loved ones.
