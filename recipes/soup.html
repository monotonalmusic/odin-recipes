Lentil Soup

Description

This lentil soup is easy to make and filling, with mostly pantry ingredients as a base.

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Carrots
  • Lemons
  • Lentils
  • Spinach
  • Vegetable Stock
  • Spinach
  • Variety of spices (tumeric, cumin, paprika)
  1. Add olive oil to the pan, on a medium-low heat.
  2. Sweat the onions and finely chopped carrot.
  3. Add the garlic, and cook for 30 seconds.
  4. Add and gently toast the spices.
  5. Add the vegetable stock and lentils, and bring to a boil.
  6. Cook for around 30 minutes, or until the lentils are almost done.
  7. Add the spinach for the last 5 minutes of cooking.
  8. Taste for salt, and add pepper if desired.
  9. When serving, add lemon juice and (optionally) fresh parsley.
